Mirco Giacobbe

I am a postdoc in the Systems Verification and Control and Verification groups, at the University of Oxford. I am interested in formal methods for safe artificial intelligence and symbolic and neuro-symbolic methods for the analysis of soft­ware and cyber-physical systems.
