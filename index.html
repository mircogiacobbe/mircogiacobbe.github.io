Mirco Giacobbe

I am an Associate Professor of Computer Science at the University of Birmingham. My research interests lie between formal me­thods and artificial intelligence. I develop automatic techniques to guarantee that algorithmic systems are safe and trustworthy by combining ideas from machine learning and automated reasoning. Here's my CV and here's a picture of me.
